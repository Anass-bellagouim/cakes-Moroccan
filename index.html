Cakes by Darcy is Atlanta’s premiere bespoke wedding cake boutique. An award-winning cake designer with over 30 years in the industry, Darcy, along with her talented daughter Andrea, are proud to be the custom cake boutique of choice in the Atlanta area since 2004. Together, we'd be honored to provide a special part of your special event, so please give us a call, email us, or fill out the contact form to begin the process of making your cake dreams become a reality!

ABOUT US

Cakes by Darcy is a family owned and operated cake boutique, established in Roswell, GA in 2004, creating bespoke custom decorated cakes for Weddings and other special occasions. ​ Darcy, An award-winning cake designer with over 36 years in the industry, and her daughter Andrea, are always honored and privileged to be asked to be a part of a client’s special day. ​ Darcy has been designing and decorating cakes since 1987, where she began working in the bakery department at a local grocery store in Marshalltown, Iowa. She then honed her craft at two Chicago landmark bakeries, before relocating to the Atlanta area in 1997, and opening her own shop in 2004. ​ Andrea, a KSU sculpture major, began her career at the age of 14, and has become an incredible asset to the business through her sculpture, many talents, dedication and amazing work ethic! ​ Darcy believes that style and creativity are her biggest assets, allowing her to take simple fresh ingredients and with Andrea’s assistance, transform them into edible works of art! ​ By booking Cakes By Darcy to create your wedding or special event cake, you can look forward to a luxury experience throughout!
